About
the
tournament

Defeat Magus Tournament in short DMT, is a speedrun tournamet created for all the people that love this game or want approach to the speedrun. Organized for the first time in 2015 by ElGranJerry, he want to bring new and old runner to do more exposure for the community. The speedrun is only 2hrs long but with a lot of strategy and glitch.

documentation

The Chrono Trigger community, expecially in the last year, has improved the route for the game. Slow and easy or faster and harder. No matter what strategy you follow. The important thing is to defeat Magus!
